How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with value +1! Try to get 25!

A little scoring note: you should have at least 402,653,184 or more points when and if you get the 25 tile. Strive for more, or even a higher tile! Can you even get the 37 tile? (It is possible; you'll need around, if not exactly, 2,611,340,115,968 points, though!)

Based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli, which was in turn based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
